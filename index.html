The Story Behind the Sound

About Aura Musics

Founder · Music Producer · Lyricist

Sri Nithis

Sri Nithis is a passionate music producer, lyricist, and the visionary founder of Aura Musics, based in Tiruchengode, Tamil Nadu. With an innate love for sound and an ear trained for cinematic storytelling, he has carved a distinctive space in the independent Tamil music landscape.

His journey began with a deep fascination for how music moves people — from the subtle pull of a background score to the resonance of lyrics that feel like lived experience. That philosophy drives every project at Aura Musics today.

A multi-disciplinary artist, Sri Nithis handles everything from writing and composing to mixing, mastering, and artist development — making Aura Musics a truly full-service creative studio for artists who believe their sound deserves no compromise.

Milestones

Studio Timeline

2019
The First Note

Sri Nithis began his musical journey in Tiruchengode, driven by obsession with sound design and the emotional power of Tamil film music. Early experiments in home recording laid the foundation for everything to come.

2021
Founding Aura Musics

The label was born from a need to give independent Tamil artists a professional platform. Aura Musics established with the mission: quality sound, honest storytelling, independent artists first.

2023
Acoustic Expansion & Isolation Revamp

Upgraded the mixing chamber with floating floors and tailored diffusors, meeting multi-channel mastering standards.

2024
Official Spotify Artist Launch

Launched Aura Musics official artist profiles on Spotify & Apple Music, crossing over half a million collective streams.

2025
Independent Artist Incubator Initiative

Launched a regional program supporting local lyrics-writers and singers with free production, tracking, and launch support.
